About

Visakhapatnam, affectionately known as Vizag, is a coastal gem nestled on the eastern shores of India, overlooking the Bay of Bengal. Blessed with a breathtaking natural landscape and a rich cultural heritage, Vizag beckons travelers with its pristine beaches, verdant hills, and bustling city life.
