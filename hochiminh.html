Ho Chi Minh City

About

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly known as Saigon, is a vibrant metropolis that pulsates with the rhythm of modernity while retaining echoes of its tumultuous history. As the largest city in Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh City serves as a dynamic hub of commerce, culture, and innovation. Set against a backdrop of skyscrapers and bustling streets, this sprawling city on the banks of the Saigon River beckons travelers with its unique blend of old-world charm and contemporary flair.
