Hanoi

About

Hanoi, the bustling capital of Vietnam, stands as a vibrant testament to the nation's rich heritage and dynamic energy. Nestled along the banks of the Red River, this enchanting city is a captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern aspirations. Known as the cultural heart of Vietnam, Hanoi offers visitors a kaleidoscope of experiences, from its ancient temples and colonial-era architecture to its bustling markets and lively street food scene.
