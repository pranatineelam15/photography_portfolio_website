Ha Long Bay

About

Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Vietnam's most iconic natural wonders, is a mesmerizing tapestry of emerald waters, towering limestone karsts, and lush islets. Nestled along the northeastern coast of Vietnam, Ha Long Bay enchants visitors with its ethereal beauty and mystical allure, inviting them to embark on a journey through a landscape that seems straight out of a fairy tale.
