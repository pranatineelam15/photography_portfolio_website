About Me

Hello!! I'm Pranati Neelam, a third-year B.Tech student hailing from the picturesque coastal city of Vizag, Andhra Pradesh. I have a deep passion for photography. I find joy in capturing the beauty of nature, the tranquillity of beaches, the intricacies of close-up shots, the vastness of the sky, and the marvels of architecture.
My journey with photography began as a hobby, but it has since blossomed into a profound love affair. Through my lens, I aim to immortalise fleeting moments, evoke emotions, and tell captivating stories. Whether it's the gentle sway of palm trees against a sunset backdrop, the delicate details of a flower petal, or the grandeur of historic landmarks, I strive to capture the essence of every scene I encounter.
Beyond the borders of my hometown, I've had the incredible opportunity to explore various European countries. Travelling has not only broadened my horizons but has also provided me with endless inspiration for my photography. Each destination has gifted me with unique perspectives, cultural insights, and unforgettable memories, all of which I eagerly translate into my work.
When I'm not behind the camera, you'll often find me immersed in my studies, pursuing my degree in Computer Science Engineering (Internet Of Things). I invite you to embark on a visual journey with me through this portfolio website. Join me as I navigate through landscapes, chase sunsets, and freeze moments in time. Thank you for stopping by, and I hope my photographs bring you as much joy as they bring me.
Happy exploring!
